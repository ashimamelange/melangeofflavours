Quick answers

Common questions

Can I request a specific recipe?

Absolutely — I love requests! Drop it in the form above or DM me on Instagram. Popular requests often become the next week's Reel.

Do you offer private cooking lessons?

Yes! 1-on-1 online sessions are available. Message me with what you'd like to learn and I'll send you details and pricing.

Are you available for brand partnerships?

Yes, I work with brands that align with accessible cooking and quality ingredients. Please send a brief and we can discuss.

I have a download issue — what do I do?

Contact me via the form above with your Gumroad order ID and I'll sort it within a few hours.

Can you host a workshop outside Bengaluru?

All online workshops are available worldwide. For in-person events outside Bengaluru, get in touch and we can discuss travel.

How do I get the Supper Club discount codes?

Sign up for my newsletter — subscribers get first access and early bird pricing for all Supper Club evenings and workshops.
