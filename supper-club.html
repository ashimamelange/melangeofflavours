The experience

What to expect

🌍

A journey through one cuisine

Each dinner is dedicated to one country. From starter to dessert, every dish tells a story about that culture's food traditions.

🛒

Pantry-only cooking

The magic of the Supper Club is the same magic as all my recipes — every dish is made without specialty stores. Accessible, delicious, surprising.

🗣

Stories behind every dish

Each course comes with the story of where it comes from, why it matters, and which pantry swaps made it possible in your kitchen.

👥

Intimate group of 8

Maximum 8 guests ensures real conversation, personal attention, and a dinner that feels like a gathering of friends — not a restaurant.

🍷

4–5 courses

A full evening's menu — amuse-bouche, starter, main, a side or palate cleanser, and dessert. All from the evening's featured cuisine.

📖

Recipe booklet to take home

Every guest leaves with a printed recipe booklet of the evening's menu — with all the pantry substitutions written in.
