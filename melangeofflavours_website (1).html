The story

Hi, I'm Ashima

I'm a food creator with an obsession for global cuisine — and a firm belief that you shouldn't need a specialty import store to cook the world's greatest dishes.

Every week I take a classic from a different culture and recreate it with pantry staples you already own. Thai, Moroccan, Japanese, Mexican — all of it, no impossible ingredient list.

This started as a personal challenge and turned into a community of home cooks around the world who just want more flavour, less fuss.
